Chapter 3: Birth of Sage Marichi

In this chapter, Brahma contemplates creation. From his divine thought emerges Sage Marichi, one of the Prajapatis ‚Äî beings tasked with continuing the cosmic lineage. Marichi becomes a progenitor of wisdom, and from him descend many revered rishis.

This chapter highlights the divine nature of knowledge and the sacred duty of sages in preserving balance. The lineage continues through Kashyapa and others, leading to countless beings that populate the worlds across time.

The act of creation is not random, but deeply spiritual and rooted in consciousness. Brahma, as the cosmic architect, breathes life into dimensions and souls through sages like Marichi.
